Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect your email address provided during sign-up for account authentication and communication purposes. We also store the messages you submit through our platform to manage and moderate community content.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your email is used to manage your account status and pending approvals. Submitted messages are processed by our system to ensure they meet community standards before being posted to our social media channels.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We utilise third-party services to operate the platform:

Supabase — authentication and database management.

Meta Graph API — facilitates posting of approved community content to Instagram.

Discord — approved messages are broadcast to our community server.

4. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the site administrator at bhhsmsgs@gmail.com.
